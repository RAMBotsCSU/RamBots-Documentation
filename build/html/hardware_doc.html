Pinouts and Hardware

IMU

The RamBOT primary functions are controlled through two core microcontrollers and an Internal Measurement Unit. (IMU)

Teensy 4.1

The Teensy 4.1 microcontroller is the RamBOTs core motion controller, communicating with the ODrives and motor encoders and utilizing the IMU for balance during operation.
